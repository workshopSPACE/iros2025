Call for Posters

We welcome poster submissions on topics related to human-robot interaction, cognitive robotics, multimodal learning, and LLMs in robotics. This is an excellent opportunity to share ongoing work, ignite discussions, and network with peers in the community.

Topics of Interest

  • LLMs and VLMs in embodied robotics
  • Multimodal perception and action understanding
  • Interactive and social robotics
  • Neurosymbolic reasoning
  • Human-in-the-loop learning and planning

Submission Details

Please submit a **1-page abstract (PDF)** along with an optional **poster preview (image or PDF)**. Submissions are not archival and are meant to foster informal exchange.

Submission Deadline: September 10, 2025

Notification: September 20, 2025

Submission email: workshop_space@outlook.com

Format

The poster session will take place during the workshop at IROS 2025. Authors will have the opportunity to present their posters and discuss their work with attendees.

We Welcome Contributions From

  • Early-stage research
  • Preliminary results
  • Ongoing PhD projects
  • Multidisciplinary efforts in cognition, language, and robotics
