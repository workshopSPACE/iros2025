About

In recent years, Large Language Models (LLMs) have revolutionized fields like information retrieval and robotics. This workshop explores the role of LLMs in collaborative physical interaction scenarios with robots, bridging natural language understanding and real-world action planning. Key themes include human modeling, benchmarking, personalization, safety, and social perception.
